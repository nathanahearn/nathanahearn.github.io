Power Automate

I have extensive experience utilizing Power Automate to provide innovative solutions for clients. Some of the key features of Power Automate that I have utilized include: Environment Variables: I have leveraged environment variables to run automations in both production and sandbox environments, providing flexibility and versatility in my solutions. Web Hooks: In one particular project, I used Web Hooks to retrieve information from a SharePoint Document Location, streamlining the process and improving efficiency. FetchXML and ParseJSON: I have utilized FetchXML to filter the list of records retrieved by the List Records function in Power Automate, providing more precise and targeted results. I have also utilized ParseJSON to access text retrieved from different fields that do not always show up in Dynamic Content, allowing me to provide even more value to my clients.
